# SportZone Demo Content

Plugin nay tao san du lieu mau cho website SportZone:

- Trang chu, Lien he, Bang size, Van chuyen, Doi tra
- Menu chinh va gan vao vi tri `Primary Menu`
- Danh muc WooCommerce: Running, Training, Football, Tennis
- 8 san pham mau, trong do co san pham noi bat
- Anh san pham mau trong Media Library. Plugin se thu tai anh mau online, neu khong duoc se tu tao anh PNG trong thu muc uploads. SVG chi duoc dung lam phuong an cuoi neu server khong co GD.

## Cach cai

1. Copy thu muc `sportzone-demo-content` vao `wp-content/plugins/`.
2. Vao WordPress Admin > Plugins.
3. Kich hoat `SportZone Demo Content`.
4. Sau khi tao xong du lieu, co the tat plugin nay.

Luu y: Hay cai va kich hoat WooCommerce truoc neu muon plugin tao san pham.

Neu san pham da duoc tao truoc do nhung chua co anh, hay tat plugin roi kich hoat lai. Plugin se gan anh cho san pham dang thieu anh dai dien.
