# SportZone Shop WordPress Theme

Theme WordPress/PHP cho website ban do the thao, toi uu de dung voi WooCommerce.

## Cai dat

1. Copy thu muc `sportzone-theme` vao `wp-content/themes/`.
2. Vao WordPress Admin > Appearance > Themes va kich hoat `SportZone Shop`.
3. Cai plugin WooCommerce neu can ban hang thuc te.
4. Tao menu o Appearance > Menus va gan vao vi tri `Primary Menu`.
5. Dat trang chu: Settings > Reading > Your homepage displays > A static page, hoac de WordPress dung `front-page.php` neu day la trang chu mac dinh.

## Tinh nang

- Trang chu ban hang cho do the thao.
- Ho tro WooCommerce, gio hang va grid san pham.
- Fallback san pham mau khi chua cai WooCommerce.
- Responsive cho desktop va mobile.
- Cac template co ban: page, post, archive, WooCommerce wrapper.
- Trang shop co hero, sidebar danh muc va product card theo layout ban hang.

## Goi y du lieu

Tao cac danh muc san pham:

- Running
- Training
- Football
- Tennis

Danh dau mot so san pham la featured trong WooCommerce de hien thi o trang chu.

## Du lieu mau tu dong

Neu muon tao san trang, menu, danh muc va san pham mau, cai them plugin `sportzone-demo-content` trong thu muc cung cap kem.
